Set apart for a weekend on the trail

Stop 3 · Pack Your Bag

What to bring on the trail

Essentials will get you through the weekend — the optional list just makes it more fun.

Essentials

  • Bible, journal and pens
  • Sleeping bag, blanket(s), and pillow
  • Toiletries
  • Towels (2–3)
  • Hat / cap
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Swimsuit (modest)
  • Warm jacket or sweater
  • Raincoat or poncho
  • Bug spray
  • Water bottle
  • Sunday best — dress to worship

Optional Extras

  • Flashlight
  • Camera
  • Sunscreen
  • Small backpack for daily activities
